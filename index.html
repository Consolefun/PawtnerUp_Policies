🐕 About PawtnerUp

PawtnerUp is a mobile platform designed to help pet owners create and join local events that foster real-world connections between pets and their people. Whether you're looking to organize a neighborhood dog walk, find a playmate for your furry friend, or connect with fellow pet lovers in your area, PawtnerUp makes it easy to create meaningful social interactions.

In addition to pet-focused gatherings, PawtnerUp also offers features to find and attend events where pets are optional—expanding possible social interactions and friendships within the pet-loving community.

Community Guidelines

Our mission is to create genuine, pet-focused connections that enhance the lives by encouraging meaningful, fun, and positive experiences in a safe and friendly environment.

📅 Our Pet-Safe Community

🌟 Our Core Values

🐾 Love for Animals

We welcome everyone who loves animals and wants to share experiences and connections through our furry friends.

🛡️ Safety & Responsibility

Every member and pets shall prioritize safety and be responsible for their actions during every interaction.

🤝 Community First

Building meaningful connections, creating local connections, fostering friendships, and creating lasting bonds.

🌈 Inclusive Environment

Respecting and celebrating all backgrounds, experiences, and walks of life while joining together to create connections.

📋 Event Guidelines

Before the Event

  • 🐾 Ensure your pet is comfortable in social situations
  • 🐾 Check local licensing and health requirements
  • 🐾 Review event details and special instructions
  • 🐾 Confirm your attendance 24 hours in advance
  • 🐾 Check if food, beverages, or treats are available

During the Event

  • 🐾 Keep your pet on leash unless stated otherwise
  • 🐾 Create your pet well and provide your own food
  • 🐾 Scoop/clean up any waste during/after your pet
  • 🐾 Be considerate to other pets and their humans
  • 🐾 Follow organizer instructions

Post-Event

  • 🐾 Leave positive reviews for good experiences
  • 🐾 Share ideas for future community events
  • 🐾 Help build community members
  • 🐾 Report any unwielcome residents
  • 🐾 Help with cleanup if available

🚫 Prohibited Behavior

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension or removal from our platform:

Unsafe Pet Handling

Bringing aggressive pets or being unkind resulting in injury/safety risks for other pets/people/handlers.

False Information

Providing false information about pets or misrepresenting illness, behavior, or personal details.

Harassment or Discrimination

Any form of harassment, bullying, or discrimination based on any characteristics is strictly prohibited.

Inappropriate Content

Inappropriate images/messages involving children or sharing unnecessary and cruel content.

Commercial Solicitation

Using events to promote commercial activities without permission from organizers or creating sales-focused unauthorized activities on the platform.

Non-Drug Policies

Possession, trading or offering about alcohol and other substance resulting in impaired behavior on events engaging.

📞 Reporting and Support

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately:

🚨 During Events

Report to event organizer immediately. If an incident is serious or dangerous for the pet/owner, call authorities if necessary.

📱 Through the App

Use the 'Report' button on the information app to let the appropriate support team know if any issues.

Emergency Contact: pawtnerup@gmail.com

For urgent safety concerns or suspected child/animal safety issues, please contact immediately through multiple platforms.

Child Safety & Exploitation Prevention

PawtnerUp is committed to protecting children and preventing exploitation.

🛡️ Zero Tolerance Policy: PawtnerUp has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE); we actively monitor and report any suspected incidents

🎯 Our Commitment

PawtnerUp is dedicated to creating a safe environment for all users, especially children. We implement comprehensive measures to:

  • 🐾 Detect and remove harmful content through automated and human review
  • 🐾 Prevent illegal content in our networks from being published
  • 🐾 Report illegal conduct to appropriate authorities
  • 🐾 Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • 🐾 Provide resources and support for child safety

🔍 Content Detection & Removal

We employ multiple layers of protection:

🤖 Automated Detection

AI-powered systems scan all uploaded content for inappropriate material

👥 Human Review

Trained moderators review flagged content

⚡ User Reporting

Easy reporting mechanisms for users to flag inappropriate content

🚀 Immediate Action

Suspected CSAE content is immediately disabled and reported

📋 Reporting & Cooperation

When CSAE content is detected, we:

  • 🐾 Immediately remove the content and suspend the account
  • 🐾 Report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • 🐾 Preserve evidence for law enforcement investigations
  • 🐾 Cooperate fully with law enforcement requests
  • 🐾 Take steps to prevent re-uploading of the same content

👥 User Safety Guidelines

To help keep our community safe:

🔒 For Parents and Guardians

  • 🐾 Monitor your child's online activity and app usage
  • 🐾 Report suspicious behavior or content immediately
  • 🐾 Supervise children under 13 when they use the platform
  • 🐾 Use privacy settings to control who can contact you
  • 🐾 Be cautious when meeting other users in person

  • 🐾 Never share personal information with strangers
  • 🐾 Meet in public places when meeting other users
  • 🐾 Trust your instincts if something feels wrong
  • 🐾 Report any inappropriate behavior immediately
  • 🐾 Don't share sensitive personal content
